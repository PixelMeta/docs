### Introduction

<Info>
  **Requires an executor**

  To execute NullFire, you need an executor, some recommended executors are: [Volcano](https://volcano.wtf/), [Wave](https://getwave.gg), [Swift](https://getswift.vip/).

  You can find more executors in [voxlis.net](https://voxlis.net/).
</Info>
